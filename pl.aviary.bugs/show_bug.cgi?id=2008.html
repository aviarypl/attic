Comment 2 Staś Małolepszy :stas 2008-11-09 11:10:20 CET 
Update Pike'a:

Hi,

you're getting this mail because I see that there's a good chunk of work left to do for your locale.

I'm speculating here, you might be waiting for string freeze for b2. The bad news is, we will break our scheduled string freeze from all I can tell. We'll be falling back to something that will be most likely be catching up with those changes on a very short time window. I'm making a stand that it's not *that* short, but it will be short.

Thus, please start hacking, commit what you have. We really want to see your work be tested in Beta 2, and gather the public feedback on the features we added since Firefox 3 or 3.1 Beta 1.

I'm sorry that we don't have better or even firm news, but I can assure you that beltzner and his team are at least as grumpy as you are on how things go compared to the plan.

If you need help, poke folks in #l10n. Many of you probably got a message or two from Stas, too, though he focused on a different metric when choosing whom to mail or not.

If you have other things going on in life that keep you from making this beta, or pose a risk for your locale making it for 3.1 final, please leave a note with me or Seth or both. I've set the reply-to to this mail to l10n-drivers to not spam your inboxes with loads of followups. Public comments should go to the .l10n newsgroup.

Thanks

Axel
