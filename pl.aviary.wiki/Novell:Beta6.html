The table presents the files translated by AviaryPL in Round Three and sent to you on 13th February. One week later, on 21st February, Zbigniew Braniecki sent you updated po files (I will refer to them as "Round Three QA"), but they didn't make it into neither Beta5 nor Beta6. The files on your FTP are from 20th February, which explains why Round Three QA has been left out.

It doesn't explain, however, why some of the files we sent you on 13th February have not been included in beta5 and beta6. The table shows that not all of our Round Three (13 Feb) files have been built into the beta releases.

What caught my attention is that the Finnish locales were rebuilt later then the Polish ones (21st February as the server says). Moreover, the yast l10n rpms on beta6 CD are from 24th February. Why was it not possible to include Round Three QA files Zbigniew sent you on 21st February?
